Team Achievements

GHC 2025

At Global Hyperloop Competition 2025, the team achieved overall victory in DesignX with the Best DesignX Blueprint Award, along with Best Subsystem Awards in Electrical and Traction, and a Special Mention in Embedded Systems, highlighting strong system integration and engineering depth. The team also secured First Runner-Up in Technical Research at InnoQuest and received the Best Documentation Award, reflecting excellence in both technical innovation and structured engineering communication.

GHC 2026

At Global Hyperloop Competition 2026, the team secured 5 trophies out of 9 entries, demonstrating strong multidisciplinary performance. The team achieved Winner titles in Mechanical and Traction Subsystems at DesignX, along with Winner awards in Vacuum Pump Technology and Stationing & Boarding at InnoQuest, and secured 1st Runner-Up in Tube Materials, reflecting excellence across mechanical design, propulsion, infrastructure, and materials engineering.
